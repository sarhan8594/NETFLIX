An aimless gamer and his two friends find themselves in a parallel Tokyo, where
they're forced to compete in a series of sadistic games to survive.
Alice in Borderland (Japanese: 今際の国のアリス, Hepburn: Imawa no Kuni no Arisu)
is a 2020 Japanese science fiction thriller drama streaming television series
based on the manga of the same name, by Haro Aso. Directed by Shinsuke Sato, the
series stars Kento Yamazaki and Tao Tsuchiya as allies trapped in an abandoned Tokyo,
where they are forced to compete in dangerous games, the type and difficulty of
which are determined by playing cards. After surviving their first game, players receive
"visas", which are extended the more they compete. If the visas expire, the individuals are
executed by red lasers being shot from the sky.
